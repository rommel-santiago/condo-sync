Welcome to Condo-Sync

Condo-Sync is a modern and intuitive platform designed to streamline communication, coordination, and management across condominium communities.

Whether you're a property manager, committee member, or resident, Condo-Sync empowers you with tools to:

  • Share announcements and notices instantly
  • Book common facilities and track usage
  • Submit and monitor maintenance requests
  • Access important documents and meeting notes
  • Stay updated with community events

Our goal is to make condo living more efficient, connected, and enjoyable.
